Our History

A legacy of faith spanning generations

1557

Early Christian Presence

Christianity was first introduced to the region by Portuguese missionaries. The area of Nandakhal became part of the early Christian settlements in the Northern Konkan region.

1858

Foundation of the Church

Holy Spirit Church Nandakhal was officially established in 1858, making it one of the historic churches in the Vasai-Virar region. The church was built to serve the growing Catholic community in the area.

1886

Parish Establishment

The church was formally established as a parish in 1886, becoming an important center for Catholic worship and community life in the Northern Konkan region.

20th Century

Growth and Development

Throughout the 20th century, the parish grew significantly, serving multiple villages and communities. The church became known for its strong community bonds and active participation in social and religious activities.

Present Day

Modern Parish

Today, Holy Spirit Church Nandakhal continues to be a vital part of the Catholic community in the Palghar district, serving the spiritual needs of its parishioners and maintaining its rich heritage of faith and service.
